About Me

I am a 4th year PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Maryland at College Park. I work in the Maryland Embedded Systems and Hardware Security (MeshSec) Lab with Dr. Gang Qu.

My areas of interest center around Security and Trust in Machine Learning and Hardware Security. I expect to graduate from the PhD program in Spring 2022.
